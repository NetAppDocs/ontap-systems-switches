= Replace FRUs - Cisco Nexus 9808
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
Your Cisco Nexus 9808 switch has field replaceable units (FRUs) that you can replace when they are faulty.


.About this task
The Cisco Nexus 9808 switch has the following FRUs:

* Fan module (6 total)
* Power supply module (2 total)
* OSFP/QSFP transceiver modules (32 fixed 400GbE ports)

.Steps

. Go to the Cisco site for the https://www.cisco.com/c/en/us/td/docs/dcn/hw/nx-os/nexus9000/9332d-gx2b/cisco-nexus-9332d-gx2b-nx-os-mode-switch-hardware-installation-guide.html?dtid=osscdc000283&linkclickid=srch&scid=srch-9332d-gx2b-hw-guide#id_111902[Cisco Nexus 9332D-GX2B NX-OS Mode Switch Hardware Installation Guide^].

. Navigate to the Replacing components section to see the FRU procedures.

. Follow the applicable procedure to replace your faulty FRU.


.What's next?

After you've completed the applicable FRU procedure on the Cisco site, you can resume normal operations.
