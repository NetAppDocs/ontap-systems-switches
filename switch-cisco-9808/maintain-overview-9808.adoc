= Maintain overview - Cisco Nexus 9808
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
Maintain your Cisco Nexus 9808 switch to ensure long-term reliability and optimal performance. You can perform maintenance tasks such as replacing faulty switch components (FRUs), replacing a faulty switch, and resetting a switch to factory default settings.

You can perform the following maintenance tasks on a switch:

* link:replace-9808-cluster.html[Replace a switch]
* link:maintain-replace-frus-9808.html[Replace FRUs]
* link:maintain-reset-switch-9808.html[Reset a switch to factory default settings]
