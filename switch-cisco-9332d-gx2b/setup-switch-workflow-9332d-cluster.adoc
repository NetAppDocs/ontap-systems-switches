= Setup switches workflow - Cisco Nexus 9332D-GX2B

:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/
After you install, cable, and power on your Cisco Nexus 9332D-GX2B switches as part of your new AFX system installation (and setup), you set up your switches by completing the initial setup, preparing to install NX-OS and RCF, installing NX-OS, installing RCF, verifying SSH is enabled, configuring cluster switch health monitoring (CSHM), and optionally, configuring Smart Call Home.

.image:https://raw.githubusercontent.com/NetAppDocs/common/main/media/number-1.png[One]link:setup-switch-9332d-cluster.html[Complete the initial switch setup]
[role="quick-margin-para"]
After you power on the switches, they begin to boot and enter the configuration wizard. You are prompted to provide answers to a series of questions to perform the initial (startup) configuration of the switches.

.image:https://raw.githubusercontent.com/NetAppDocs/common/main/media/number-2.png[Two]link:verify-cabling-9332d-cluster.html[Verify switch cabling]
[role="quick-margin-para"]
After cabling the switches in the AFX system and completing the initial switch setup (which happens automatically after power on the switches), verify the cabling of the switches to ensure that each switch is connected properly to the other switch and to the storage system. Proper cabling is required for the switches to operate correctly in your AFX system.

.image:https://raw.githubusercontent.com/NetAppDocs/common/main/media/number-3.png[Three]link:install-nxos-overview-9332d-cluster.html[Prepare to install NX-OS and RCF]
[role="quick-margin-para"]
Prepare to install the NX-OS software and RCF on the switches.

.image:https://raw.githubusercontent.com/NetAppDocs/common/main/media/number-4.png[Four]link:configure-software-overview-9332d-cluster.html[Install the software]
[role="quick-margin-para"]
Install the NX-OS software on the switches.

.image:https://raw.githubusercontent.com/NetAppDocs/common/main/media/number-5.png[Five]link:configure-software-overview-9332d-cluster.html[Install the RCF]
[role="quick-margin-para"]
Install the RCF on the switches.

.image:https://raw.githubusercontent.com/NetAppDocs/common/main/media/number-6.png[Six]link:configure-software-overview-9332d-cluster.html[Verify SSH is enabled]
[role="quick-margin-para"]
Verify SSH is enabled so that you can remotely manage the switches...configure health monitor.

.image:https://raw.githubusercontent.com/NetAppDocs/common/main/media/number-7.png[Seven]link:../switch-cshm/cshm-overview.html[Configure switch health monitoring]
[role="quick-margin-para"]
Configure the switch health monitoring (CSHM) to ensure the operational health of your switches and for the collection of switch logs for debugging and troubleshooting.

.image:https://raw.githubusercontent.com/NetAppDocs/common/main/media/number-8.png[Eight]link:smart-call-9332d-cluster.html[Configure Smart Call Home (optional)]
[role="quick-margin-para"]
Configuring Smart Call Home on your switches is optional. It is used to monitor the hardware and software components on your network.

[role="quick-margin-para"]
